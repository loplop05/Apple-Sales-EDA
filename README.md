# Apple Jordan Sales EDA

An end-to-end exploratory data analysis project built in Jupyter Notebook, using a fictional sales dataset derived from the Apple Jordan (ABJ) product catalog. All transaction figures and salesperson performance data are synthetically generated for educational purposes.

---

## Overview

This project simulates a full year of Apple product sales in Jordan (FY 2024) and performs a structured analysis across product categories, monthly trends, salesperson performance, and key business KPIs. The dataset is generated programmatically from a real ABJ SKU catalog, with realistic pricing in Jordanian Dinar (JOD) and seasonality based on Ramadan, Eid, Back-to-School, and iPhone launch periods.

- iPhone 11 and above (iPhone 11, 12, SE)

## What the Notebook Covers

1. Environment setup and library imports
2. Synthetic data generation from the ABJ product catalog
3. Data overview and quality check
4. Monthly sales figures broken down by product category
5. Salesperson performance analysis with KPI comparison
6. Best-selling and least-selling product rankings
7. Best and worst months for sales
8. Key performance indicators (KPIs) including quarterly breakdown, price tier analysis, and YoY growth estimate
9. Monthly sales report cards
10. Executive dashboard and strategic recommendations

---

## Visualizations Included

- Stacked bar chart: monthly revenue by category
- Line chart: total monthly revenue trend
- Horizontal bar charts: salesperson revenue, units, and average transaction value
- Line chart: monthly revenue per salesperson
- Heatmap: category revenue mix per salesperson
- Bar charts: top 10 and bottom 8 products by revenue
- Pie chart: revenue share by category
- Bar chart: best and worst months highlighted
- Bar charts: quarterly revenue and units
- Box plot: revenue distribution by category
- Bar chart: revenue by price tier
- Correlation matrix
- Executive summary dashboard
